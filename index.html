Frequently asked questions

What is Disney+?

+

Disney+ is the destination for the Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic, Star and + universes.

What can I watch on Disney+?

+

Disney+ offers an ever-expanding collection of stories:

  • Exclusive original series and films
  • Timeless classics from Walt Disney Animation Studios
  • Entertainment stories with comedies, dramas, stories inspired by real-life events, etc.
  • National Geographic documentaries, special productions and much more.

How much does a Disney+ subscription cost?

+

You can choose from three Disney+ subscription levels:

  • Premium (11.99 $/month or 119.99 $/year) : Resolution up to 4K Ultra HD and HDR. 4 simultaneous playbacks. Downloads for offline playback on up to 10 devices. Dolby Atmos sound on compatible content.
  • Standard (8.99 $/month or 89.99 $/year) : Resolution up to 1080p Full HD. 2 simultaneous playbacks. Downloads for offline playback on up to 10 devices. 5.1 surround sound on compatible content.
  • Basic (5.99 $/month) : Resolution up to 1080p Full HD. 2 simultaneous playbacks. 5.1 surround sound on compatible content. Advertising content.

What devices and platforms can I use with Disney+?

+

Disney+ is available on mobiles, web browsers, game consoles, set-top boxes and connected TVs.

Is there a commitment period for Disney+ subscriptions?

+

Disney+ is non-binding and you can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation will be effective
at the end of the period for which you have paid.

  1. Log on to www.DisneyPlus.com
  2. Select your profile
  3. Select Account
  4. Select your subscription
  5. Select Cancel subscription
  6. Select Continue to cancel
